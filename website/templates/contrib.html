Connect your Station

If you have a running Ground Station and would like to connect it to the this network, just do it!

  1. Go to the access section in this page and register your account within the system.
  2. You will have wait for the administrator to validate your account after you have verified your email.
  3. Install the generic client on the machine to which you regularly plug in your radio or modem.
  4. Once your account is activated, you will connect your software to the servers of the SatNet network by using your registered account

In case you have a CubeSat flying and you want to use the network to operate it with remote Ground Stations, you can register your account and use the generic client to exchange data frames with the network.
